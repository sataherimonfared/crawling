# CMWS Water Days 2026

Call for Abstracts

Scientific Programme


### Contact

cmws@desy.de

We are excited to announce that the CMWS Water Days 2026 will take place from Monday, February 23 to Wednesday, February 25, 2026 at the DESY campus in Hamburg.

Following this, the CMWS Research Course on “Water-Driven Materials” will be held from Wednesday, February 25 in the afternoon to Thursday, February 26, 2026, also at the DESY campus.

# CMWS Water Days 2026, February 23-25

The conference will begin with a plenary session providing updates on the status of CMWS and will continue with invited and contributed talks . We would also like to take this opportunity to invite you to join the pillar meetings on Tuesday afternoon . On Tuesday evening, you are warmly invited to join us for the Poster Session .

## Call for contributed talks(extended until 16.01.26)!

Following the many excellent contributions from last year, we would again like to invite contributed talks (15+5 minutes) from all of you to further strengthen knowledge exchange and foster networking. To express your interest in giving a talk, please upload your abstract with your tentative title by January 9, 2026 . The option to submit your title and abstract can be found on the left column of the page or at the buttom of the page . The submission option can be found in the left-hand column of the page.

The selected speakers will be notified by January 21, 2026.

We are also delighted to announce that the CMWS Water Days will once again include a public evening lecture on Wednesday, February 25 th, 2026, at 7 PM in the DESY Auditorium (Building 5) . The keynote lecture “ Die Wasseroberfläche in Subnanometer Auflösung,“ will be delivered by Roland Netz (FU Berlin) in German. We warmly invite you to join us for this public event.

Confirmed Invited Speakers CMWS Water Days 2026 :

Frédéric Caupin (Université Claude Bernard Lyon 1)

Oliver Plümper (Universität Bremen)

Francesca Toma (Helmholtz Zentrum Hereon)

Herma Cuppen ( Radboud University Nijmegen)

# CMWS Research Course 2026, February 25-26

On Wednesday afternoon, as part of our program, we especially invite Ph D students, Postdocs, and interested scientists to participate in the CMWS Research Course on Water: “Water-Driven Materials” , which continues until February 26 . The course will feature lectures on topics such as fluid transport materials, mechanical materials, propagation and scattering of light and photonic materials, as well as energy materials.

Speakers of the CMWS Research Course on Water: “Water-Driven Materials”

Vasily Artemov (Technische Universität Hamburg)

Michael Fröba (Universität Hamburg)

Patrick Huber (Technische Universität Hamburg / DESY)

Robert Meißner (Technische Universität Hamburg)

Alexander Petrov (Technische Universität Hamburg)

Alexander Schlaich (Technische Universität Hamburg)

Shan Shi (Technische Universität Hamburg / Helmholtz Zentrum Hereon)

Berit Zeller-Plumhoff (Helmholtz Zentrum Hereon / Universität Rostock)

Imke Greving (Helmholtz Zentrum Hereon)

Please note that registration is not yet open as we are still finalizing some administrative details. We will send a follow-up email as soon as registration becomes available. As last years, a registration fee will apply.

Please note: This is an onsite, in-person meeting.

For questions, contact us via cmws@desy.de.
