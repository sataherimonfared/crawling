## Welcome to the Kickoff for VISOR!

VISOR is the Hamburg VISTA School of Graduate Research. VISOR provides co-funding for projects on the topic of development or application of AI techniques in the natural sciences and engineering, initiated by pairs of supervisors associated with Hamburg VISTA from different institutions. During this kickoff, the projects that have been selected for funding will be presented, along with exciting talks on AI in biology and medical research. We finish the evening with networking over beer and brezels.
