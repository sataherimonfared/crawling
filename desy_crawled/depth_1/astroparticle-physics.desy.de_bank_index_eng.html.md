## Investitionsbank des Landes Brandenburg (ILB)

Support for scientific instrumentation and technical machinery has been provided by the ILB.  The grant was awarded by a Directive of the Ministry of Science, Research and Culture (MWFK) for the Promotion of Infrastructure for Research, Development and Innovation from the ERDF (Infra FEI).  The funding has supported instrumentation in the Detector, Electronics and Mechanics groups.

Group Leader: Steven Worm

Total Budget: 384.305 EUR
