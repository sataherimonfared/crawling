## DESY events for the science community

Events in the DESY calendar are primarily aimed at researchers and students.


### Visiting DESY

Please book your tour directly at DESY’s locations in Hamburg or Zeuthen via the following pages. DESY’s guided tours are not published in the DESY calendar.

### DESY tour in Hamburg

DESY invites you on a journey into the world of the smallest particles.

More about the DESY tour in Hamburg

### DESY tour in Zeuthen

More about the DESY tour in Zeuthen

### Sources of information

More DESY events for researchers and everyone else can be found on the following pages:

### DESY Science Café

Science, maths and technology are fun. The DESY Science Café is a meeting place for anyone who wants to talk about it – online or on site at the DESY campus.

More about the DESY Science Café

### DESY CONNECT

DESY CONNECT is a young but constantly growing alumni network that is open to all former and current DESY employees and colleagues on campus.

More about DESY CONNECT

### Science City Hamburg Bahrenfeld

DESY is a partner of Science City Hamburg Bahrenfeld. Tours, concerts and many other events in Hamburg’s new district for science can be found on the following website:

More events on sciencecity.hamburg
